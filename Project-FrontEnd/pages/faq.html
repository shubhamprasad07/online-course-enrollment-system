Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions below.

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, simply go to the "Courses" section, select a course, and click "Enroll". You will be automatically enrolled in the course.

Can I switch courses after enrolling?

Yes, you can switch courses by contacting our support team within the first week of enrollment for assistance with course transfers.
